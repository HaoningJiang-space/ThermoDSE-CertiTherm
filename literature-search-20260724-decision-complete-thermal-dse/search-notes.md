# Search Notes

## Safe queries used

- thermal-aware chiplet design-space exploration architecture power temperature
- robust optimization decision-dependent information discovery exact algorithm
- counterexample-guided abstraction refinement hardware verification
- sensor placement decision robustness value of information
- chiplet accelerator hardware mapping co-optimization
- temperature-aware multi-chip-module sizing DATE
- query minimization cheapest set under uncertainty
- adaptive submodularity information gathering

Only public topic terms and public paper titles were used. No unpublished
result text, local path, or private proposal wording was submitted as a query.

## Sources checked

- arXiv paper pages
- official DATE proceedings archive
- official NeurIPS proceedings
- IJCAI proceedings
- JMLR
- ACM/DOI and Springer/DOI records
- IEEE publication metadata surfaced through stable institutional records
- ScienceDirect and INFORMS records for optimization work

## Excluded sources

- Policy-excluded venues and domains were omitted from the report.
- Reddit, Wikipedia, commercial paper summaries, and untraceable PDFs were not
  used as evidence.
- ResearchGate results were ignored when a primary or stable source existed.
- Search snippets without an inspectable paper record were not included.

## Unknowns

- A fully exhaustive DAC/ICCAD/FMCAD search has not yet been completed.
- ThermoDSE is a recent public preprint; no peer-reviewed venue status is
  inferred.
- Monad venue metadata should be checked from its final publisher record before
  manuscript citation.
- Patent and commercial EDA-tool prior art has not been searched.
- No industrial report-cost dataset was found in this pass.

## Handoff notes

### For idea optimization

Keep decision-complete EDA abstraction as the core. Generic information
discovery, CEGAR, and sensor placement are strong neighboring literatures, so
the physical EDA property and evidence must carry novelty.

### For direction scouting

High-value follow-up searches:

- abstraction adequacy or fidelity selection in EDA DSE;
- multi-fidelity DSE with costly physical analysis;
- formal guarantees for surrogate-assisted architecture selection;
- decision-focused optimal experimental design under polyhedral uncertainty;
- industrial EDA report latency and fidelity hierarchies.

### For experiment design

Use thermal/chiplet DSE papers to define realistic candidate frontiers and
mapping couplings. Use DDID, robust observation selection, and decision
robustness as algorithmic baselines or theorem boundaries, not as directly
comparable thermal systems.
